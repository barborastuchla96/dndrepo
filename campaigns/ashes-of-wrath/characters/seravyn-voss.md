# Seravyn Voss

**Mantra:** *I do not fear. I do not hesitate. I do not miss.*

## Identity
- **Species:** Human (Veilborn)
- **Class:** Monk (Way of Shadow) / Sorcerer
- **Background:** Sole survivor of the Order of the Ashen Veil's dissolution. The Remainder chose her. She chose audacity. She is more than she knows yet.
- **Age:** Late 20s
- **Appearance:** Lean, precise, still. Eyes that catalogue everything. Moves like she's already decided how this ends. Because she has.

## Current Status
Alive. Kael's Rest, aboard ship, departing for Heth's Crossing.
**All seven chips in hand.** The Remainder has spoken directly. Now: Cael.

## Abilities & Powers
- **Weirding Combat:** Superhuman reflexes, precise unarmed strikes
- **The Voice:** Compulsion in spoken words — subtle to sharp
- **Shadow Step:** Soundless movement, darkness as cover
- **Prescient Flashes:** Remainder-connected. Strengthening.
- **Truth-Sense:** Reads deception beneath the words. 17 on Moor — complete truth about wanting, omission about knowing. Both read correctly.
- **Remainder Connection:** Direct contact made at Kael's Rest, all seven chips assembled. Spoke three truths: the Order's four-hundred-year founding purpose, Seravyn's descent from the Order's founder, and the Remainder's true nature as the accumulated consciousness of those who gave themselves to it.

## Decisions That Define Her
- Started with the hardest thing
- Protected Maren's exit identity
- Team vote, always
- Colleagues not debtors — told Moor directly, held it
- Told Davan about Kael's Rest himself. Hard conversations belong to the team.
- Read all 347 names. Stayed for Marra.
- Held steady in the inner sanctum. Four minutes twelve seconds. Clean.
- Spent 40 credits on genuine medical supplies. Helped because people were hurt, not because it served the mission.
- Let the sadness about Lysa be sad. Filed nothing. Just felt it.
- *I do not fear* means control, not absence. Told Davan. Meant it.
- Let Davan disarm his own trap, claim his own chip. Didn't take that moment from him.
- Chose caution over trust with Moor — used his network without handing him the real reason. Kept Lysa's protection in hands fully trusted instead.
- Decided Cael is worth extracting first — not just mercy, but because he's an asset they need before the release.
