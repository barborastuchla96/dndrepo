# Seravyn Voss

**Mantra:** *I do not fear. I do not hesitate. I do not miss.*

## Identity
- **Species:** Human (Veilborn)
- **Class:** Monk (Way of Shadow) / Sorcerer
- **Background:** Member of the Order of the Ashen Veil — dissolved under mysterious circumstances. Sole survivor.
- **Age:** Late 20s
- **Appearance:** Lean, precise, still. Eyes that catalogue everything. Moves like she's already decided how this ends.

## Abilities & Powers
- **Weirding Combat:** Superhuman reflexes, precise unarmed strikes, reads opponents before they move
- **The Voice:** Can layer compulsion into spoken words — subtle suggestions, sharp commands
- **Shadow Step:** Blends into darkness, moves without sound
- **Prescient Flashes:** Rare, unbidden glimpses of immediate futures — not reliable, never comfortable
- **Truth-Sense:** Detects emotional tells and micro-deceptions; cannot be lied to easily

## Stats (D&D 5e)
- **Level:** 3 (Monk 2 / Sorcerer 1)
- **HP:** 24 / 24
- **AC:** 16 (Unarmored Defense)
- **Ki Points:** 2 / 2
- **Spell Slots:** 1st level x2

## Equipment

- Two short blades, plain-hilted

- 40 credits (near broke)
- A small obsidian token, the only thing she took when she ran

## Notes
- Sole survivor of the Order of the Ashen Veil's dissolution
- Does not know who ordered it or why she was spared — or targeted
- Currently operating without allegiance, which makes her both free and hunted
- Has not used her full name in over a year
