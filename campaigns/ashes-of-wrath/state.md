## Session 1 — The Ember Port

**Date:** 2026-06-15

## Current Situation
Seravyn has been in hiding for eleven months since the Order of the Ashen Veil was dissolved in a single night. She doesn't know who gave the order. She doesn't know why she survived. She knows someone is still looking for her.

She has surfaced in **Karath's Drift** — a lawless transit station at the edge of Imperial space. The kind of place where names are optional and questions are expensive. She's been here three weeks, doing ugly work for ugly people, keeping her head down.

That ends today.

---

## The World: Ashes of Wrath

### The Galactic Setting
The **Valdric Imperium** spans four hundred inhabited systems. It is old, vast, and rotting. The Emperor — **Aurek Valdric III** — is a figurehead. Real power is fractured across:

- **The High Synod** — noble houses who fund the Imperial war machine and expect returns
- **The Crimson Compact** — the Imperium's intelligence and enforcement apparatus; brutal, efficient, loyal only to itself
- **The Mechanic Guilds** — control interstellar travel; officially neutral, actually the most powerful faction alive
- **The Remnant Orders** — ancient mystical traditions including the now-destroyed Order of the Ashen Veil

### The Veilborn
The Order of the Ashen Veil was old — older than the Imperium. They served as advisors, negotiators, and shadows. They were tolerated because they were useful and feared because they were dangerous. Eleven months ago, every Veil sanctum in the known galaxy was hit simultaneously. Hundreds dead. The Order's archives wiped. Official Imperial statement: *"Terrorist cell neutralized."*

No one believes it. No one says otherwise.

### Karath's Drift
A rusted transit hub orbiting a brown dwarf. Population: ~12,000 permanent, thousands more passing through. Run by **Station Boss Emrik Haul** — a former Compact enforcer who bought his way out and bought this station in. He keeps order through monopoly on the airlocks and a willingness to space people who cause problems.

Factions present:
- Compact informants (unknown identities)
- Guild factor **Tessian Moor** — watching all inbound/outbound travel
- Refugee cell from a destroyed noble house (House Vorrath — they lost a war three years ago)
- **The Unmoored** — a loose criminal network Seravyn has done small jobs for
