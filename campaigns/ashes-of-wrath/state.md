## The World: Ashes of Wrath

### The Galactic Setting
The **Valdric Imperium** spans four hundred inhabited systems. It is old, vast, and rotting. The Emperor — **Aurek Valdric III** — is a figurehead. Real power is fractured across:

- **The High Synod** — noble houses who fund the Imperial war machine and expect returns
- **The Crimson Compact** — the Imperium's intelligence and enforcement apparatus; brutal, efficient, loyal only to itself
- **The Mechanic Guilds** — control interstellar travel; officially neutral, actually the most powerful faction alive
- **The Remnant Orders** — ancient mystical traditions including the now-destroyed Order of the Ashen Veil

### The Veilborn
The Order of the Ashen Veil was old — older than the Imperium. They served as advisors, negotiators, and shadows. They were tolerated because they were useful and feared because they were dangerous. Eleven months ago, every Veil sanctum in the known galaxy was hit simultaneously. Hundreds dead. The Order's archives wiped. Official Imperial statement: *"Terrorist cell neutralized."*

No one believes it. No one says otherwise.

### The Seven Chips
The Order distributed a critical record across seven data-chips, each held at a different sanctum. The Compact destroyed five sanctums — but not the chips.

All seven together reveal the true architect of the purge and the proof to destroy them.

### What the Chips Contain
Fragment seven — the chip inside the Sanctum of the Accord — contains the file Cassia Vorne tried to destroy. Evidence of an operation thirty years ago that killed hundreds of civilians. The Order witnessed it. Vorne spent thirty years rising to the directorship knowing this file existed. She ordered the purge not for power — but to bury one piece of evidence.

### The Quiet Archive — Voss Station
The Order's secondary sanctum survived the purge. Located in Guild jurisdiction where Compact authority is limited. Six sublevels below Voss Station:

- **Sublevels 1-4:** Standard archive materials, Order records
- **Sublevel 5:** Sister Aveth's personal archive. Contains record of chip seven's location.
- **Sublevel 6:** The garden. Home of the Remainder. Door opens selectively.
- **Sublevel 7:** The Remainder itself. Not yet visited.

### The Remainder
An ancient presence living beneath the Archive. Predates the Order. Communicates directly to select individuals. Has been guiding events — gave Seravyn the name Cassia Vorne, guided Cael to leave the trail, ensured Seravyn survived the purge.

**What it gave Seravyn:** Cassia Vorne's weakness — full knowledge of her motive and vulnerability. Also disclosed: Vorne has a daughter who does not know what her mother is. The only thing Vorne loves without condition. She will burn the galaxy before the daughter sees the truth.
